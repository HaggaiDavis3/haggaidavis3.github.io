Greening the Last Mile: How Innovative Delivery Methods Benefit Everyone

Learn about new strategies, programs, and technologies that combat congestion by moving freight cleaner, faster, and safer. As last-mile deliveries continue to increase and Chicago looks to assert itself as a freight leader, the city should expand its toolkit for effectively managing the rise in congestion caused by these vehicles. Fortunately, Chicago is not alone, as many other major cities globally are facing similar challenges. By being proactive, this burden can be converted into an opportunity that enables continued economic growth and job creation. Join us for an evening of insights and networking as we explore the forefront of innovation in managing the movement of goods in dense urban areas. Delivered in Chicago at Arcadis on 21 August 2025. Introduction to Neural Networks (NNs): How and When to Use Recurrent NNs and Convolution NNs

Machine learning is an incredibly broad term that encompasses a lot of different algorithms that can be applied to solve a variety of problems. Neural networks are one category of ML algorithms that consist of layers of nodes (or neurons) which will activate to transmit information when certain thresholds are met. RNNs and CNNs are subclasses of neural networks whose differing structures allow for the analysis of different types of datasets, usually things like time-series and pattern recognition, respectively.
